## Pillar 5 — Logo Suppression Index
Quiet luxury is often defined by what is absent.

Track:

- subtle logos  
- loud logos  
- “new money vs old money” patterns  
- industry differences (tech vs fashion vs finance)

## Pillar 6 — Tailoring Evidence Archive
Tailoring is one of the most consistent billionaire casual signals.

Track:
- hem precision  
- sleeve length  
- shoulder fit  
- pant break  
- waist shaping  
- consistent proportions  

This pillar teaches:
- what tailoring costs  
- what tailoring changes  
- how to tailor on a budget  
- how to spot quality fit

## Pillar 7 — The Family Layer (Secondary + Minimal)
Coverage of husbands/boyfriends and children is secondary.

Husbands/partners:

- only in public event contexts  
- never obsessive  
- used only to analyze generational taste transmission
