Featured Uniform

The Quiet Luxury Airport Look

The most documented billionaire casual uniform in our archive. Spotted on at least 7 billionaire women in Q4 2025 alone. The formula: oversized cashmere sweater in oatmeal + tailored wide-leg trousers in charcoal wool + Hermès Birkin 35 in étoupe + Loro Piana cashmere travel wrap + Common Projects sneakers in white. No logos visible anywhere. Total: ~$18,400.

Why it signals wealth: The cashmere is baby-grade Mongolian (you can feel the weight), the trousers have been altered at least twice (the break is perfect), and the bag has a natural patina that says "I've had this for years." Nothing is new. Everything is maintained.
